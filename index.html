Her Story

A life lived in full colour

Rose Adeyemi raised five children with grace, patience, and a warmth that made every room feel like Sunday morning. She was a teacher, a cook, a storyteller — and above all, she was home.

As we gather to celebrate her life, we invite family, friends, and all those whose lives she touched to contribute to the costs of laying her to rest with the dignity she deserves.

Your generosity — in any amount — is a testament to the love she planted in the world.

"She taught us that kindness was not a gift you gave — it was a garden you tended."
